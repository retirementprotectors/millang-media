The Opportunity

Most local tradespeople either have no website or a terrible one from 2012. They know they're losing jobs to the guy who shows up first on Google — they just don't know how to fix it.

Millang Media builds professional, mobile-first websites for local trades — starting with plumbers in the Des Moines metro. One killer template per niche. Swap the content. Launch in 48 hours. Charge monthly.

10 Growth clients = $10,000 setup + $2,500/month recurring. That's $30,000/year in recurring revenue from 10 clients. 20 clients = $60K. The setup fees fund the build time. The monthly fees compound.

Talan's Daily Playbook

This is what you do every day to build the business. No code required. Just calls, demos, and follow-ups.

Cold Call Script

INTRO: "Hey [Owner Name], my name is Talan with Millang Media. I'm a local web designer here in Des Moines — I actually found your business on Google and noticed you don't have a website yet. Is that right?" IF YES: "Yeah, I figured. A lot of plumbers in the area are in the same spot. I actually specialize in building websites specifically for plumbers — mobile-friendly, shows up on Google, has a click-to-call button right at the top. I can actually show you a demo of what yours would look like. It takes about 2 minutes. Would you be open to that?" IF THEY SAY "HOW MUCH?": "It starts at $500 for a single-page site, and I handle everything — design, hosting, updates. There's a small monthly fee for hosting and support, but no contracts. Cancel anytime. Want me to show you the demo?" IF THEY WANT THE DEMO: "Perfect — pull up your phone if you can and go to..." (Send them the Heartland Plumbing demo link via text) "See how that looks? Now imagine that says [Their Business Name] instead of Heartland Plumbing. That's what I'd build for you." CLOSE: "I can have your site live within 48 hours. If you want to get started, I'll send you a quick link to get the setup fee taken care of and we're rolling. Sound good?"

Demo Walkthrough

When showing the Heartland Plumbing demo on your phone or theirs:

1
Start at the top — "See that emergency banner? That's live 24/7. And this Call Now button? One tap and it dials you. 80% of your customers are on their phone."
2
Scroll to reviews — "I'd pull your real Google reviews right onto the site. You've got [X] reviews at [X.X] stars — that's your best sales tool and right now nobody sees it unless they're already on Google."
3
Show the map — "Your service area, right here. Customers see you're local, see exactly where you're based."
4
Contact form — "They can request service right from the site. You get an email with their name, number, and what they need. No phone tag."
5
The close — "Everything you just saw? I can have that live with YOUR name, YOUR number, YOUR reviews in 48 hours. And that Millang Media credit at the bottom means I handle all the tech."

Prospect Prioritization

Work the list from the top. Your hottest leads are businesses with:

  • High Google rating (4.5+) — They're doing great work, they just need online presence
  • High review count (50+) — Busy business, money to spend, understands customer feedback
  • No website — Obvious. They need you.
  • Bad website — Harder sell but bigger opportunity. "I can make this way better."
