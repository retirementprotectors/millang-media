Our Story

Des Moines Born.
Des Moines Built.

Heartland Plumbing was founded in 2008 by Mike and Tom Kowalski — two brothers who grew up in Windsor Heights and apprenticed under their father, a journeyman plumber for 30 years in Polk County.

What started as a two-truck operation out of a garage on Hickman Road has grown into a 12-person team serving over 2,400 Des Moines homeowners annually. We haven't changed our mission since day one: show up on time, fix it right, and leave the place cleaner than we found it.

We're members of the Iowa Plumbing, Heating, and Cooling Contractors Association and hold an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau. We've been recognized as a "Best of Des Moines" plumber four years running by the Des Moines Register reader poll.

Our Promise to Every Customer

Every technician we send to your home is a licensed journeyman or master plumber — not an apprentice working alone, not a subcontractor we hired yesterday. We carry $2 million in general liability insurance, and every job is backed by a one-year labor warranty. If we fix it and it breaks again within a year, we come back at no charge.

We charge flat rates, not hourly. You'll know the price before we touch a wrench. No surprise invoices. No hidden fees. That's how we've earned a 4.9-star rating across 200+ Google reviews.
