🏠 Our Story

Des Moines Locals
Since 1987

Heartland Plumbing was founded by Dale Kowalski out of the back of a single service van in 1987. A second-generation plumber from Ames, Iowa, Dale's philosophy was simple: treat every customer's home like it's your own.

Over 37 years, that philosophy scaled. Today, Heartland operates a fleet of 14 trucks, employs 23 licensed plumbers, and has completed over 12,400 residential and commercial jobs across the greater Des Moines metro.

We're not a franchise. Every technician is a W-2 employee who's been background-checked, drug-tested, and trained by our lead master plumber. When Heartland shows up, you know exactly who you're getting.
